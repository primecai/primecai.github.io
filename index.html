Shengqu Cai

I am a Computer Science master student at ETH Zürich supervised by Prof. Luc Van Gool. I am currently visiting Stanford University at the Computational Imaging Lab led by Prof. Gordon Wetzstein.

I am interested in using artificial intelligence to solve tasks that are fundamentally ill-posed via traditional methods, slay the unslayable. I have been working primarily around neural rendering and 3D vision, including but not limited to generative models, unsupervised learning, GAN inversion, scene representations, visual content creation, etc.
